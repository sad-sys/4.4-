About Us

We are a community dedicated to being the first in providing up to date news on the Jurassic Park franchise

  • 1990 The first Jurassic Park book was released
  • 1993 Jurassic Park was adapted into film form
  • 1997 The first Jurassic Park fan website was developed, documenting the development of the franchise
  • 2003 The inspiration behind this site, jurassicpark4.com was set up
  • 2015 The fourth Jurassic Park film, Jurassic World was released leading to a reawakening in the Jurassic Park franchise
  • 2020 Jurassic Times began development as a website to coincide with the Jurassic Park Franchise
